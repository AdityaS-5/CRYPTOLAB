🔑 RSA Key Generation

Generate RSA public and private key pairs

ℹ️ How it works:

  • p and q: Two large distinct prime numbers
  • n = p × q: The public modulus
  • φ(n) = (p-1) × (q-1): Euler's totient function
  • e: Public exponent (usually 65537)
  • d: Private exponent, calculated using extended GCD
  • Public Key: (e, n)
  • Private Key: (d, n)
